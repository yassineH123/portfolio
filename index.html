À propos

Hi! I’m Aya Zerouali, an undergraduate fashion design student at Casa Moda Academy in Morocco. I’m passionate about transforming creative ideas into tangible designs, blending fashion with graphic art to create bold, meaningful pieces.

My design style draws from diverse cultures and aesthetics, capturing life’s little details and turning them into cinematic memories. I approach every project with curiosity, adaptability, and a drive to learn from new challenges.

I believe fashion is more than clothing — it’s a story told through texture, color, and form. I’m currently seeking opportunities to showcase my work, connect with visionary minds, and grow as both a designer and a storyteller. One of my biggest ambitions is to gain international experience, whether through further studies or professional opportunities, to immerse myself in diverse creative environments and push the limits of my imagination.
